Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Banana bread is a classic quick bread that’s moist, soft, and full of sweet banana flavor. It’s a comforting treat that can be enjoyed for breakfast, as a snack, or with tea and coffee.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mash the ripe bananas in a mixing bowl.
  3. Stir in melted butter, then add sugar, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in baking soda, salt, and flour until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before slicing and serving.

Enjoy!
