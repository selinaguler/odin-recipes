Virgin Piña Colada Recipe

Description

Virgin piña colada is a refreshing tropical drink made without alcohol, blending creamy coconut milk, sweet pineapple juice, and crushed ice into a smooth, frosty beverage. It’s a popular non-alcoholic option that captures the same tropical flavor as the classic cocktail, perfect for hot days or festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk or cream of coconut
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Fresh pineapple slices (for garnish, optional)
  • Maraschino cherry (for garnish, optional)

Steps

  1. Add pineapple juice, coconut milk, and ice cubes to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass.
  4. Garnish with a pineapple slice and cherry, if desired.

Enjoy!
